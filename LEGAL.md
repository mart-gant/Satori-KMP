# Legal Transparency & AI Usage Disclosure

## Project Overview
Satori is a personal well-being assistant designed for neuroatypical users. It utilizes a combination of traditional software logic and Artificial Intelligence (Gemini AI) to provide recommendations and insights.

## AI Usage Policy
To mitigate intellectual property risks and ensure compliance with copyright standards, we maintain a clear distinction between human-originated code and AI-assisted generation.

### 1. Source Code Origination
- **Core Architecture:** The architecture (KMP, Clean Architecture principles) is defined and implemented by human developers to ensure ownership of the project's structural IP.
- **Business Logic:** Critical business logic in `/shared` and `/server` is manually audited and refactored by human developers to maintain intellectual property integrity.
- **UI Components:** Generative AI is utilized as a productivity tool for boilerplate UI components in `/composeApp`, with final validation and creative modification by human developers.

### 2. AI Content Generation
- **Insights:** Personal well-being insights are generated dynamically by Gemini AI based on user-provided data. These insights are clearly labeled as AI-generated in the UI.
- **Recommendations:** Recommendations are derived from a hybrid logic of manual heuristics and AI analysis.

### 3. Data Privacy & GDPR
- User data processed by Gemini AI is anonymized where possible.
- AI features are opt-in, requiring explicit user consent before any data is sent to external AI providers.

## IP Ownership Statement
All structural code, original algorithms, and custom branding elements are the exclusive property of the developers. AI tools are used strictly as a non-creative assistance layer (similar to a compiler or advanced IDE), ensuring that the copyrightable "originality" remains human-originated.
